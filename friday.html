About NISAB 2.0

NISAB 2.0 is a student-focused training organised by NIFES to help young people develop practical and profitable skills beyond the classroom.

Participants will learn directly from experienced instructors through projects, workshops, assignments, and live practical sessions.

Available Skill Tracks

Frontend Web Development

Learn HTML, CSS, JavaScript, responsive design and modern web practices.

Graphic Design

Master branding, typography, posters and visual communication.

Video Editing

Learn cinematic editing, storytelling and motion graphics.

UI/UX Design

Understand wireframing, prototyping and interface design.

Mobile App Development

Build Android and cross-platform applications.

Digital Marketing

Learn content marketing and audience targeting.
